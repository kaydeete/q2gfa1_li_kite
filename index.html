Save Coral Reefs
Coral reefs advocacy involves raising awareness about the critical importance of these ecosystems for marine biodiversity and human well-being, emphasizing the urgent need for conservation measures to address threats such as climate change, overfishing, and pollution. By promoting sustainable practices, international cooperation, and policy initiatives, advocates strive to safeguard and restore coral reefs, preserving their ecological integrity for current and future generations.
